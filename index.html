📜 About Myself

Hi, I'm Ivana Yael Currá. After more than 10 years of working as a Project Manager for over 20 companies, they decided not to hire me anymore.
So, I decided to make a career change.
I had the opportunity to contribute to the world of entertainment during 2021 and go on and partially see myself through the eyes of others and remember everything I lost in my memory about myself.
In 2023, I started my career as an actress. It didn’t go too well, to be honest, but now I see a world full of possibilities.
In 2024, I didn't give up acting and I re-started my learning at UNA,
I returned to practicing taekwondo ITF, started cooking near my home
I am still part of UADE as an adjunct teacher. In the Faculty of Design and Architecture, the director has decided not to give a space to continue teaching at UADE due to a performance rating of 52%/80%.
Without further support, I am uncertain about what will happen in 2025 at UADE.
However, in 2025, I am planning to study design and development in video games formally at Universidad de la Ciudad de Buenos Aires and I finally going to start my brand officialy and 100% on rule online and I have plans to make it bigger, little by little.
