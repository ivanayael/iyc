📜 About Myself

Hi, I'm Ivana Yael Currá. After more than 10 years of working as a Project Manager for over 20 companies, they decided not to hire me anymore.

So, I decided to make a career change.

I had the opportunity to contribute to the world of entertainment and partially see myself through the eyes of others.

In 2023, I started my career as an actress. It didn’t go too well, to be honest, but now I see a world full of possibilities.

I returned to practicing taekwondo, started cooking near my home, and I am planning to study design and development in video games.

I am still part of UADE as an adjunct teacher. In the Faculty of Design and Architecture, the director has decided not to give a space to continue teaching at UADE due to a performance rating of 52%/80%. Without further support, I am uncertain about what will happen in 2025.

However, I am finally going to start teaching online for real this time.

🏆 Personal Development and Achivements

2023

I’ve become a better person. I’m still working on that. Sometimes, it’s hard to look through a window with a smile instead of a neutral or panicked expression.

I managed to finish some courses this year, though not at my best performance. I need to work on paying more attention in class, but it was better than 2022 and definitely better than 2021 and 2020, when I struggled with a memory problem.

I got promoted to half red belt in Taekwondo. I can't believe I got that far. But I still need to work on stretching a lot. I didn’t perform the wood-breaking part with the right technique.

I took some courses at UADE, so I can still go there, but it wasn’t my best self.

I started studying at UNA but didn’t pass the entrance exam. I have to retake it in February 2025. I do exercises every day, even if it's just for a minute, but I did something.

I had the chance to be a speaker again at the Meet and Net event, and I also created the website and the web app for the "sorteo."

I had the opportunity to be in a music video and a movie as an extra, better than I imagined.

I got a part in a play of The Addams Family adapted in the city of La Plata. It was great, even though I don’t know how to dance and can’t sing the higher notes. No one has called me again for another play.

I still don’t have motivation, self-esteem, the will to keep going, and, most importantly, self-love, and I’m still looking for it.

I hope one day I’ll find the answer.
